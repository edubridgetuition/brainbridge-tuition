Setup Phase Step 1 of 9

⚙️ Standard Listings (Add Classes)

This is the foundation of your entire setup. Define your tuition classes (e.g., 10th Std) and their monthly fees.

🗺️ Where to go?

Click on Settings ⚙️ in the sidebar ➜ select the Fees Structure tab at the top.

❓ Why is this important?

Without adding a Standard (e.g., 10th Std), you cannot create batches for it, register students under that grade, or generate monthly fee invoices.

📝 Checklist (How to do it)
  • Standard Name: Enter the grade name (e.g., 6th, 10th Science, JEE prep).
  • Board Name: Enter the board (e.g., CBSE, ICSE, GSEB).
  • Monthly Fee: Input the standard fee charged to students in this class.
  • Click Add Standard to save.
💡 Pro-Tip

If you charge different fees for streams (e.g., Science and Commerce) in the same grade, create them as separate Standards: "12th Science" and "12th Commerce".

Setup Phase Step 2 of 9

👨‍🏫 Register Teachers & Staff

Add teachers and staff to let them log in, take attendance, and assign homework.

🗺️ Where to go?

Go to the Staff Management 👥 page in the sidebar ➜ click the "+ Register Staff" button at the top.

❓ Why is this important?

Adding your teachers allows you to assign them to specific batches. Teachers will only see academic records for the batches they teach.

📝 Checklist (How to do it)
  • Enter the teacher's name, email ID, and mobile number.
  • Set a temporary login Password (teachers can change this after logging in).
  • Designation / Role: Choose their role (e.g., Teacher, Admin).
  • Permissions: Select what actions they are allowed to perform.
Setup Phase Step 3 of 9

⏰ Create Batches (Study Groups)

Create timings/slots (batches) and link them to tuition classes.

🗺️ Where to go?

Go to the Students 👥 page in the sidebar ➜ click the "+ Create Batch" button at the top.

❓ Why is this important?

Linking a Batch to a Standard (Class) means that when you assign homework, select a student, or view timetables, selecting the Batch automatically auto-populates the Class/Standard field, saving you from double data entry!

📝 Checklist (How to do it)
  • Batch Name: Enter a unique identifier (e.g., 10th Morning Batch, 6th Science Slot B).
  • Subject: Enter the subject taught (e.g., Mathematics, Physics).
  • Standard/Class: Select the grade level created in Step 1.
  • Class Timing: Specify the time slot (e.g., 04:00 PM - 05:00 PM).
  • Teacher Name: Assign the teacher responsible for this batch.
Daily Operations Step 4 of 9

🧑‍🎓 Register Students (Admissions)

Register students and assign them a class and batch.

🗺️ Where to go?

Go to the Students 👥 page in the sidebar ➜ click the "+ Register Student" button at the top.

❓ Why is this important?

Students must be assigned to a Batch to appear in attendance sheets, receive homework notifications, and pay standard class fees.

📝 Checklist (How to do it)
  • Enter the student's name, roll number, and parent's WhatsApp number.
  • Standard (Class): Choose their grade from the dropdown (e.g., 10th).
  • Batch: Select their specific study group/batch.
  • Click the Register Student button to save.
Daily Operations Step 5 of 9

📅 Daily Attendance & WhatsApp Alerts

Mark batch attendance and send parent alerts on WhatsApp.

🗺️ Where to go?

Click on Attendance 📅 in the sidebar.

❓ Why is this important?

Notifying parents of student absence immediately improves reliability, trust, and attendance rates at your academy.

📝 Checklist (How to do it)
  • Select the Batch and the current Date at the top.
  • Mark each student as Present or Absent.
  • Click Save Attendance to sync with the cloud.
💬 WhatsApp Absent Alerts

Once saved, click the WhatsApp Icon next to any absent student to send a pre-configured alert message directly to their parent.

Daily Operations Step 6 of 9

📝 Assign Homework

Assign homework to batches (Class is auto-detected).

🗺️ Where to go?

Go to the Homework 📝 page ➜ click the "Assign Homework" button at the top.

❓ Why is this important?

With our latest update, selecting a Batch automatically detects and fills the Class / Standard field! This saves time and avoids mistakes.

📝 Checklist (How to do it)
  • Batch: Select the target batch.
  • Class / Std: Automatically filled upon selecting the batch.
  • Homework Title: Enter the topic/exercise name.
  • Instructions: Write out clear instructions for the student.
  • Image Attachment (Optional): Upload a photo of the questions (max 50KB).

Billing & Analytics Step 8 of 9

💰 Fees Collection & WhatsApp Reminders

Collect monthly fees, generate receipts, and send WhatsApp reminders.

🗺️ Where to go?

Click on Fees 💰 in the sidebar.

❓ Why is this important?

The system auto-calculates monthly dues based on the student's assigned Standard fee. Marking fees paid generates printable PDF receipts and updates financial logs instantly.

📝 Checklist (How to do it)
  • Review the list of students with pending dues.
  • Click "Collect Fee" when a payment is received, choose the payment mode (Cash, UPI, Card), and confirm the collection.

For any pending fees, click the WhatsApp Icon next to the student's name. The app automatically drafts and sends a message containing the unpaid balance and payment instructions to the parent's phone!

💡 Standard vs Batch Explained

Understanding this distinction will save you from double-entering data into the ERP:

1. Standard (Class / Grade level)

This is the school grade (e.g. 6th Grade, 10th Science, JEE prep). It defines the base Monthly Fees. You set this up once in Settings.

2. Batch (Class Time & Subject)

This is the group/time slot where students sit and study (e.g. 10th Morning Maths, 10th Evening Physics). Every Batch is linked to a Standard.

Why link them? Once a Batch is linked to a Standard, you only select the Batch during homework assignment or student registration. The system automatically fetches and sets the correct class level without asking you again!
